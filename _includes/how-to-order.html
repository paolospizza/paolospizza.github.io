How to Order

  • Click on our Menu and decide which delicious Pizza's you would like to order! You can also check our Facebook page to see if there are any daily specials.
  • Choose your delivery day and preferred delivery slot
  • Our standard delivery days are Wednesday – Saturday* (5pm - 9pm)
  • Please note that the days we are open for delivery may vary due to holidays and other occurrences. Keep an eye on our Facebook page for all updates to opening hours.
  • Our delivery slots are every 20 mins starting at 5pm through till 9pm (eg. 5pm, 5.20pm, 5.40pm, 6pm etc.)
  • Call us on {{site.telephone}} to place your order.
  • We will take a card payment, (plus your delivery address and a contact number), then your order will be booked in!
  • Our telephone line is open for orders between 11am and 10pm, Weds – Sat. Outside of those hours, please feel free to leave a message and we will call back to take your order when someone is available.
  • N.B During delivery hours (5pm-9pm) we are liable to be very busy and may not be able to answer the phone, but will get back to you as soon as we can.
  • Look forward to the arrival of your delicious Pizza!
  • We will aim to be as close to your agreed delivery slot as possible, but as all our pizzas are hand-made to order, please allow (approx.) 15 mins leeway either side of your delivery time.
