Menu About Paolo's How to Order

Paolo’s Pizza is a new Pizza delivery service based in Marple, UK.

We offer delicious, hand stretched Pizza, made in the authentic Neapolitan style and delivered directly to your door.

When you can’t go to the Pizzeria, let the Pizzeria come to you!

Paolo's Pizza is a Micro-Pizzeria which means we only make (approx.) 30 Pizzas a day. With such a limited number available, we suggest always booking your order in advance.

Ordering Hours: Weds – Sat, 11am - 10pm

Delivery Hours: Weds – Sat, 5pm - 9pm

N.B During delivery hours (5pm-9pm) we are liable to be very busy and may not be able to answer the phone, but will get back to you, as soon as we can, to take your order.
