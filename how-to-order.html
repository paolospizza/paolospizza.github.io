Menu About Paolo's How to Order
Paolo's Pizza is a Micro-Pizzeria which means we only make (approx.) 30 Pizzas a day. With such a limited number available we recommend, where possible, booking your order in advance.

How to Order

  • Click on our Menu and decide which delicious Pizzas you would like to order!
  • Remember to check our Facebook page for weekly specials, promotions, and any changes to our weekly opening hours due to holidays etc.
  • Choose your delivery day and preferred delivery time-slot.
  • Delivery days are Thursday, Friday, & Saturday.
  • Delivery slots are every 20 mins from 5pm till 9pm
    (e.g. 5pm, 5.20pm, 5.40pm, 6pm etc.)
  • Call us on 0161 427 0607 any time between 11am and 10pm, Wednesday-Saturday to place your order. Remember, ordering in advance really helps us as a business and guarantees you the delivery time-slot that you want.
  • We will take a card payment, (plus your delivery address and a contact number), then your order will be booked in!
  • Look forward to the arrival of your delicious Pizza!
  • Please allow (approx.) 15 mins leeway either side of your quoted delivery time.
